Featured Collection

TruNorth

TruNorth celebrates the raw beauty of real wood—nothing fabricated, nothing forced. Featuring TruTexture, each 8 1/2″ wide White Oak plank reveals the natural story of the tree it came from: surface contours shaped by air drying, and rich tones deepened through fuming. No artificial scraping, just organic character refined through thoughtful craftsmanship. With every board uniquely its own, TruNorth offers a truly authentic hardwood floor—timeless, modern, and rooted in nature.
